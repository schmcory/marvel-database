Description

This is a database representing supernatural humans, humanoids, aliens, and other creatures from the Marvel Comic Universe. This is a universe with superheroes and villains. The database contains beloved characters and lesser known ones. Anyone who is familiar with movies like Iron Man or Spider-Man will understand this universe well. You can download the database's files here. This database was inspired by Marvel Wikia.


    Entities

    Heroes

    Here you can list, add, update and delete persons (perform create, read, update and delete operations on a hero and filter by home planet and search by alias).

    Villains

    Here you can list, add, update and delete persons (perform create, read, update and delete operations on a villain and filter by enemy and search by alias).

    Planets

    Here you can list, add, update and delete planets (perform create, read, update and delete operations on a planet and search by name).

    Abilities

    Here you can list, add, update and delete abilities (perform create, read, update and delete operations on an ability and search by name).

    Teams

    Here you can list, add, update and delete teams (perform create, read, update and delete operations on a team and search by name).


    Relationships

    Heroes-Teams

    Here you can change the team a character is a member of (many to many).

    Heroes-Abilities

    Here you can change the abilities a character has (many to many).
